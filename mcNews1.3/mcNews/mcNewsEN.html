mcNews 1.3

Description

- Public section

The homepage ( index.php ) shows to the guest a navigation bar with three links:
list (list.php)
search (search.php)
submit (ecrire.php)
Below this bar the x last News are shown in detail:
Date, title and author, followed by icons / links towards the site and the email of the author if he indicated them in the ecrire.php form. The site opens in a new window, and the email sends to a contact form mailto.php, the address email remaining so invisible. One finds then the text of the News, then until three links followed by their description. Then the guest can read comments (lire.php) if there's, or to write one (comment.php).


List displays the xx last News, classified by date, with subject and author. A click on one of them sends to the page read.

Search suggests a research in all News with one to three words-keys, the operator AND or OR, and the possibility looking for in the authors, the titles or the texts. The result appears as the page list.

Submit is the form to submit aNews. No limitation, every guest can propose one News, but it must be validated by you before being published. The required fields are: Name, Title and Text. Email is optional, but must be valid if filled. It's possible to insert until three links and their description at the end of text, and four smilies are automatically managed: :)  :(  :grr: and ?!? are replaced by    and . HTML tags aren't recognized, avoiding nasty tricks. You receive an email informing you about the submission of a News and inviting you to go to validate it.
Privilege administrator: by typing admin in the field name and by validating, you're redirected to the reserved form (see lower).

Read one or several comments displays the News concerned and below all the comments added by the readers. Comments appear as News, the title less.
The same privilege for the administrator as above.

Write thus allows to comment a News. As submit, every guest can post a comment, but you've to validate it so that it's published. The characteristics of the comment form are the same that those of News, and you're also warned by mail.


- Section administration

Once crossed the login page, several choices:

Design parameters (design.php). You choose here the appearance of mcNews (only for the public part) according to the files which are in the skin directory. You'll find there only two for the moment, up to you to write one matched with your site. And especially, please, think of sending them to me, I shall give them to all.

Technical parameters (tech.php). It's here necessary to choose the language, in the same way as for skins, files being in the lang directory. The same demand of my part, please, send your translations! You also have to fill the other fields. The name is the one you want to see appearing in "author" on your News, the email is for the warning of post and the site URL for the link which allows you to leave the admin after logout. The number of News on homepage corresponds among News "displayed" that you want to see appearing (generally from 5 to 10, but the choice is free, attention at the speed because of the number of requests), the number of News in page list can be much bigger, as far as it also determines the number of possible answers in search.

News waiting for validation shows you the list. To click one sends you to the page accept modify delete (valid.php )

Comments to be validated: here are listed News already validated but which received a new comment. The links send you to the same page as above.

News validated is the list of all News for which there's nothing again. Please, use it todelete the oldest News, to avoid the clogging of the data base. Always the same page.

Publish a News, at the top to the right, opens you a form pre-filled with your name and email address (fixed in the technical parameters). In this form you can use HTML tags, to write in colours, in fat, even insert images or links into the text. Everything is possible, even the errors! Thus, please, be careful. On the other hand Administrator's News are validated immediately.

Accept Modify Delete displays a News and its eventuels comments. Click accept to validate it, modify opens a form allowing you to modify everything, and to add HTML tags if you want. One News which you modify must be then validated, the accepted word is then red. Same procedures for comments. Delete a News also deletes all its comments, delete a comment deletes only itself. You can also from this page write a comment, and as administrator it enjoys privileges already seen higher.
Installation


First, edit the config.inc.php file and fill variables.
$Admin_login and $admin_pass will be your login and password to the administration of mcNews.
Then, upload the complete directory mcNews on your Web server .
Connect and navigate until http: // www.votresite/... / mcNews / admin / install.php,
... and please, follow simply the instructions.
