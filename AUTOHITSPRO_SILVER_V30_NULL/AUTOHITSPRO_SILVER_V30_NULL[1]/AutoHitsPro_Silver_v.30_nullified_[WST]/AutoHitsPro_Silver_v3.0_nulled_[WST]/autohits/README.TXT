



INSTALLATION
==============================================================================

a) Upload all files to your server
b) chmod the following:
     error_inc.php - 777
     config_real_inc.php - 777
      prices1.inc.php - 777
     prices.inc.php - 777

     /admin/timer_inc.php - 777
     /admin/error_inc.php - 777
    /admin/prices1.inc.php - 777
    /admin/prices.inc.php - 777


c) goto http://www.yourdomain.com/install.php in your browser
    and follow the set up instructions, then create database.

d) login to your admin area setup - http://www.domain.com/admin/setup.php
     and set up the site.

e)  goto http://www.domain.com/admin/prices.php and set up your sites prices
      and set the timer for your autohits site. ** Silver - You can set Autohits or Surf 4 Hits here also**

f) goto: http://www.domain.com/admin/prices1.php and set up your membership prices.


 that is all, you can now customize your sites looks and feel by editing the pages below:::



EDIT FILES TO MATCH YOUR SITE
==============================================================================
 'header_inc.php'
 'footer_inc.php'
 'faq.php'
'hits.php' 
 'contact.php'
 'adv.php'
'autohits.css' to manipulate the layout and additional texts for your site.
