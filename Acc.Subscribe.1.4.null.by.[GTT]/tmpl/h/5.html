Bulk sending
On this screen you could select subscribtions and categories what should be emailed, also you could select period where user was registered.

To track undelivered e-mail you should set POP3 account on `Settings` screen.
All undelivered e-mail will be processed.

In `recent e-mail` section you could select messages, that you already sent.


Also on this page you could see sent e-mails statistics.
