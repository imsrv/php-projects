Chat-Help

Directions for use:

  • Menu "Channel_1 ()": Change of channel
  • Button "Twaddle filter": You get only text displayed, that from or to you written. Your nickname appears then in parentheses. Thus other Chatter can see that you activated the twaddle filter.
  • Button "Status filter": Hide all text lines like 'Billy wispers to Bobby'.
  • Menu "say to / speak to": Speak or wisper directly to a chatter.

  • Menu "Chat messages" Here you can leave messages for other chatters.
  • Menu "Ignore/Invite": Here you can ignore or invite chatters.
  • Private room: The channel with the name like your nickname is your private channel.
  • red nickname: These users are godfathers of the chat.

IRC-Commands

  • /nickname some other text To speak to an other chatter.
  • /me Is replaced by your own nickname
  • /msg nickname some other text To wisper to an other chatter.
  • /join channel name Changes the channel
  • /locate nickname Checks, if an user is currently online in the chat

If you have your own icon (a 16x16 Pixel-GIF) in the chat you can call it by typing ':me', other users must speak to you in order to call with ':you' your icon.
