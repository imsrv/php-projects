Info:
  ABC Catalog is an easy-to-use managing     
  catalogs program that allows you to publish 
  on your web-site various data such as       
  products catalogs, price-lists,             
  specifications stored in Excel tables.      
  All you need is to select an Excel file     
  on your local computer and press the [Parse]
  button.                                     
  ABC Catalog lets you adjust look-and-feel  
  properties of your generated HTML(color and 
  width of table border, font and background  
  color for category headers).
